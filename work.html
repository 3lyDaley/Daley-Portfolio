Run Buddy

Run Buddy is a homepage for a mock start-up with the intention to find local runners and connect them to a run-buddy personal trainer. Client's request was to create an attractive webpage, complete with a contact form, functioning navigational links to hop to specific areas of the page, and a separate page with the company's privacy policy. The site is equipped with mobile queries to ensure compatibility with screens of all sizes.

Horiseon

Horiseon, a mock-client, requested their homepage be refactored to update their accessibility and reorganize their code for maximum effiency and legibility. All navigation links were checked and/or repaired for long term use.
